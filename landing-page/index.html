Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI understand my products?

Our app uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision to analyze your product images, extracting details like colors, materials, styles, and unique features to create accurate descriptions.

Can I edit the generated descriptions?

Absolutely! You can review and edit any generated description before publishing. You can also create custom templates to match your brand voice.

Does it work with existing products?

Yes! You can generate new descriptions for existing products in your store, not just new ones.

What about SEO?

All descriptions are SEO-optimized with relevant keywords. The app also extracts Google Shopping metafields automatically.

How fast is generation?

Most descriptions are generated in 20-30 seconds. Bulk processing allows you to queue multiple products.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You get 24 free descriptions to test the app. No credit card required.
