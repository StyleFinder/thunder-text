# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about Thunder Text.

## General Questions

### What is Thunder Text?

Thunder Text is a Shopify app that uses AI to generate product descriptions automatically. It analyzes your product images and information to create compelling, SEO-friendly descriptions that help sell your products.

### How does the AI work?

Thunder Text uses OpenAI's advanced language models (GPT-4 Vision) to:
1. Analyze your product images
2. Review product title, category, and existing data
3. Generate natural, persuasive descriptions
4. Format content for Shopify

### Do I need technical skills to use Thunder Text?

No! Thunder Text is designed for non-technical users. If you can use Shopify, you can use Thunder Text. Just click "Generate" and review the results.

### Can I try before buying?

Yes! Thunder Text offers a free trial (check current offer in Shopify App Store). You can generate descriptions and test all features before committing to a paid plan.

## Billing and Pricing {#billing}

### How much does Thunder Text cost?

Pricing is available on the Shopify App Store listing. Plans typically include:
- **Free Trial**: X descriptions to test the service
- **Starter**: XX descriptions/month
- **Professional**: XXX descriptions/month
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited with priority support

*Note: Check the App Store for current pricing*

### How are generations counted?

Each time you click "Generate Description," it counts as one generation, regardless of whether you publish it. Regenerating the same product counts as a new generation.

### What happens if I exceed my limit?

- Your existing descriptions remain active
- You can't generate new descriptions until:
  - Next billing cycle starts, OR
  - You upgrade to a higher plan

### Can I change plans?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm - changes take effect immediately

Pro-rated credits apply when upgrading.

### Do you offer refunds?

- **Free Trial**: Cancel anytime, no charge
- **Paid Plans**: No refunds for partial months
- **Billing Issues**: Contact support@thundertext.com

## Content Quality {#quality}

### How accurate are the AI descriptions?

AI descriptions are generally 85-95% accurate but should ALWAYS be reviewed before publishing. The AI may:
- Misinterpret complex technical details
- Miss brand-specific terminology
- Generate creative but inaccurate claims

**Always review and edit before publishing.**

### Can I customize the tone/style?

Yes! In Settings you can configure:
- **Tone**: Professional, casual, enthusiastic, etc.
- **Length**: Short (50 words), medium (100 words), long (200+ words)
- **Focus**: Features, benefits, storytelling
- **Keywords**: Add SEO terms to include

### Will descriptions be unique?

Yes! Each description is generated fresh and unique. We don't use templates or copy descriptions between products.

### Can Thunder Text write in different languages?

Currently, Thunder Text generates descriptions in English only. Multi-language support is on our roadmap.

### What if I don't like a generated description?

You have several options:
1. **Regenerate**: Click "Regenerate" for a new version
2. **Edit**: Modify the description inline
3. **Discard**: Delete and try again
4. **Mix**: Keep parts you like, regenerate others

No limit on regenerations within your plan.

## Technical Questions {#technical}

### What permissions does Thunder Text need?

Thunder Text requires:
- **Read Products**: View your product catalog
- **Write Products**: Update descriptions when you click "Save"
- **Read Shop**: Access store name and settings

We do NOT access:
- Customer data
- Order information (unless needed for analytics)
- Payment information
- Unrelated shop data

### Is my data secure?

Yes! Thunder Text:
- ✅ Encrypts all data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- ✅ Encrypts data at rest (AES-256)
- ✅ Uses Shopify's secure authentication
- ✅ Complies with GDPR and data protection laws
- ✅ Deletes your data within 48 hours of uninstallation

See our [Privacy Policy](../../PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

### Does Thunder Text work with all Shopify plans?

Yes! Thunder Text works with:
- Shopify Lite
- Basic Shopify
- Shopify
- Advanced Shopify
- Shopify Plus

### Can I use Thunder Text on multiple stores?

Each Shopify store requires a separate Thunder Text installation and subscription. Bulk pricing available for 5+ stores - contact support@thundertext.com.

### What happens to my descriptions if I uninstall?

- Descriptions already published to Shopify remain in your store
- Draft descriptions in Thunder Text are deleted
- Your account data is deleted within 30 days per GDPR
- You can reinstall anytime and start fresh

## Integration Questions

### Does Thunder Text integrate with other apps?

Thunder Text works with standard Shopify products. It's compatible with most Shopify apps, but specific integrations include:
- ✅ Shopify Products (native)
- ✅ Shopify Collections
- ⏳ More integrations coming

### Can I import/export descriptions?

- **Import**: Not currently supported
- **Export**: Yes! Download generated descriptions as CSV from Settings

### Does it work with Shopify's bulk editor?

Yes! Descriptions generated by Thunder Text appear in Shopify's bulk editor and can be edited there.

## Usage Questions

### How long does generation take?

- **Single Product**: 5-15 seconds
- **Bulk (10 products)**: 1-2 minutes
- **Large Bulk (50+ products)**: 5-10 minutes

Time varies based on:
- Image complexity
- API response time
- Current system load

### Can I generate descriptions for products with no images?

Yes, but quality may be lower. The AI will use:
- Product title
- Existing description (if any)
- Category/tags
- Variant information

**Recommendation**: Add at least one image for best results.

### Can I schedule bulk generation?

Not currently. Bulk generation is manual. Scheduled generation is on our roadmap.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Uninstall Thunder Text from your Shopify admin
2. Your data will be automatically deleted within 30 days
3. For immediate deletion, email support@thundertext.com
